About the Event

"Binary Battles" is more than a competition—it's a celebration of the relentless curiosity and innovation inherent in the field of technology.

Organized by the prestigious Yogananda School of AI, Computers, and Data Science, this annual event challenges aspiring tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills across a variety of engaging and intellectually stimulating rounds.

Participants will experience a gamut of activities—from algorithm showdowns and coding marathons to data analysis face-offs. We augment the competitive spirit with enriching workshops and insightful keynotes from industry stalwarts.

This event is not just a battleground for the sharpest minds but also a fertile ground for seeding networks, nurturing ideas, and celebrating the sheer joy of problem-solving in the digital realm. All while vying for the prestigious title of Binary Battle Champion and significant prizes.
