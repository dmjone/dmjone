Multi-Modal Knowledge Integration System (MMKIS)

Application No. 202511010666 • Filed on 11 Jan 2025
Patent pending

Plain-English summary. This invention describes a human-centered learning interface that blends gentle, non-intrusive multi-sensory cues with adaptive visual presentation to help learners maintain focus, pace their sessions, and keep continuity between on-screen and printed study. The approach is privacy-minded and avoids collecting unnecessary personal data.

  • What it aims to achieve: sustained attention, lower cognitive load, clearer review—online and offline.
  • What it is not: no medical/therapeutic claims; no specialized hardware required.
  • Why it matters: accessible study experiences that respect attention and privacy.

Inventors. Divya Mohan · Prof. Pankaj Vaidya · Dr. Bharti Thakur
