Art-Integration and Indian Knowledge Systems are worth 5 marks inside English itself — that is the Subject Enrichment row, and the Do and Connect modes here are built to feed it. Quiz, Talk and Listen feed Multiple Assessment. CBSE states that roughly 50% of questions are competency-focused (case-based, source-based, integrated, data-interpretation, situational, application-oriented), and that at least one periodic assessment must be competency-based. NCF-SE 2023 asks that Grade 10 boards "be based on the competencies set for each of the curricular goals in that area". Every mode here names its competency.

On DAV: DAV CMC prescribes its own textbooks and value-education books (Naitik Shiksha, All About Vedic Dharma) for LKG to Class VIII only — its 2026-27 circular says so in as many words. Class 9 English is governed by CBSE and NCERT, and CBSE names Kaveri as the textbook. This portal follows CBSE/NCERT because that is what actually binds Class 9.
