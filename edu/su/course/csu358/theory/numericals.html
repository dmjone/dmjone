Q8. Rotate Point (5,2) by 90°

About Origin:

$$x' = x \cos 90^\circ - y \sin 90^\circ = x(0) - y(1) = -y$$ $$y' = x \sin 90^\circ + y \cos 90^\circ = x(1) + y(0) = x$$

Substitution:

  • $x = 5, y = 2$
  • $x' = -2$
  • $y' = 5$
  • Result: $\mathbf{(-2, 5)}$

Q9. Rotate Triangle A(1,1), B(2,3), C(3,1) by 45°

$\cos 45^\circ = \sin 45^\circ = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \approx 0.707$

Formulas:

  • $x' = 0.707x - 0.707y$
  • $y' = 0.707x + 0.707y$
