Guide to Sorting Options

Course Code: Sorts alphabetically / numerically by the course identifiers of Shoolini University.

Course Name: Lists courses based on their names, alphabetically.

Easier First: Begins with courses considered less challenging.

Harder First: Starts with the more challenging subjects.

Fewer Topics: Courses with fewer topics come first for a gradual introduction.

More Topics: Tackles courses with a broader range of topics from the start.

Early Semesters: Organizes courses as they appear through the academic timeline, starting.

Later Semesters: Lists courses starting from those offered in the final semesters.
