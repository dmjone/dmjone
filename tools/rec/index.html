Slower system? We offer online.vbs, a visual basic script, which works completely in the background, capturing a screenshot every 7 seconds for 2 hours once it's activated.

How to use the online.vbs: You can download the script from the given link above or directly by clicking here. Before running the script, ensure you are connected to the internet and that your system has a drive labeled 'C'. For users frequently working on offline systems, we recommend downloading the standalone version onto a portable device like a pen drive. Once either of these versions are downloaded, run the downloaded script, and proceed with your work you want to screenshot. No interface is displayed after the 1st prompt. This script will record your work in form of screenshots from the background, saving screenshots every 7th second in the designated C:\autoscr\ folder. The script will automatically stop two hours from the time it started. To stop the script before the end of the two-hour span, simply re-run the script file, log out of your user profile and log back in, or or shutdown or restart your system.
