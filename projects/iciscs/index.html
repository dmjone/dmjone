ICISCS 2024

About Conference

The revered Yogananda School of Artificial Intelligence Computers and Data Sciences, integral to the illustrious Faculty of Engineering and Technology at Shoolini University, Solan, Himachal Pradesh, India, has the honor of hosting the esteemed International Conference on Intelligent Systems Computing and Security (ICISCS 2023) within the university's prestigious walls. This conference is scheduled to take place on the 20th - 21st December 2023.


Held in the heart of our esteemed institution, this conference seeks to foster international collaboration in the rapidly evolving disciplines of computer science, data science, artificial intelligence, security, and information technology. The event is tailored to encourage dialogue and cross-fertilization of ideas among a diverse range of participants including eminent researchers, students, industrialists, developers, and practitioners from around the globe.


Recognized as a frontrunner in multimedia applications and real-time computing, ICISCS 2023 is committed to advancing scientific knowledge. Emphasizing the critical domains of data science, machine learning, and real-time computing, it aims to galvanize progress in the development of robust, innovative systems.


Leveraging the university's world-class facilities, the conference will orchestrate a series of enlightening plenary talks, dynamic workshops, and parallel technical sessions. It's our shared mission to stimulate the evolution of vital technologies, tools, architectures, and infrastructures, paving the way for a prosperous future marked by collaborative solutions to global societal and economic challenges.

Conference Themes

Network & Information Security

Delving into the encrypted labyrinth of cryptography and network security, this theme champions the protection of information systems. Join us as we fortify the digital fortress against unauthorized intrusions and preserve the integrity of our data.

Artificial Intelligence

An exploration of the intersection between human intellect and machine efficiency, our theme on Artificial Intelligence is all about algorithms, machine learning, and intelligent systems. Embark with us on a journey into the realm where machines learn and evolve.

High-Performance Computing

Journey into the world of High-Performance Computing, where we push the boundaries of computational power and speed. Here, we explore technologies capable of processing vast amounts of data quickly and efficiently, opening the doors to unprecedented possibilities.

Internet of Things

With Internet of Things, we knit a digital tapestry of interconnected devices that revolutionize our lifestyle and work. Smart grids, home automation, wearable systems, and more—step into a reality where everything is truly within your reach.

Big Data Analytics and Business Intelligence

Harnessing the power of Big Data, we extract the needle of valuable insights from the haystack of information. Cloud and edge intelligence, predictive analytics, and more come together in this theme to transform business operations and strategies.

Emerging Trends in Computer Science

Embrace the next wave of innovation with Emerging Trends in Computer Science. Image & Audio processing, Robotics, Block Chain, and more await to revolutionize your digital perspective. Journey with us into the future of technology.

Sustainable Futures

Step into the tomorrow we strive to create today—a Sustainable Future. From innovation in agriculture to smart cities, from renewable energy to e-waste management, we explore it all in the pursuit of a greener, cleaner, and healthier world.

Mathematical Sciences

Immerse yourself in the elegant complexity of Mathematical Sciences. This theme encapsulates everything from advanced mathematical theories to statistical techniques, mathematical modeling, and their applications. Join us as we decode the numerical fabric of the universe.

Call For Papers

Interested Research Scholars, Faculty Members, Scientists and Engineers working in Research Institutes, Universities, Colleges, and Industries related to the field of conference themes and allied branches may submit the Abstract (200 words). All the submissions will be processed through EasyChair conference management system.

Corresponding / Presenting Author(s) have to register themselves for the International Conference, without which the paper will not be considered for presentation.

Publication

All accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings indexed in Scopus/WoS, based on the suitability of manuscript as recommended by the reviewers.

About Shoolini

Founded by leading academicians and professionals, Shoolini University is a young, research-focussed, philanthropic university. Established in 2009, this dynamic and innovation-led centre of learning has made giant strides. In 2023 as per Times Higher Education rankings, the University was ranked as the No.1 private university in India. It has consistently been ranked among India's top 100 universities (NIRF), is one of India's highest generators of patents and innovation and matches the world's best universities in research output. The University is accredited by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC). The University is located in Solan, a city in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, known as the "Mushroom city of India," situated between Chandigarh and Shimla on the Kalka-Shimla National Highway-5, at an altitude of 1,550 m above mean sea level, and 45.5 km south of the state capital, Shimla.

Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering & Technology at Shoolini University was given a 'Diamond Rating' in the prestigious QS I-GAUGE rankings in 2021 for excellence in qualitative teaching, robust learning, and proactive research. The faculty has two schools namely, School of Core Engineering and Yogananda School of AI, Computing and Data Sciences.

How to Reach

The Nearest airport is at Chandigarh and Shimla, and the nearest broad gauge railway station is at Kalka, while the closest narrow gauge rail station is at Solan. The university is 15 km from Solan, 60 km from Shimla, and 70 km from Chandigarh.
