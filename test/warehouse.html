Waits for CPU < 40%, then bumps its own priority. Dedupes via a SHA-256 hash of all original fields. Appends transformed rows (with a GUID) to your warehouse CSV. Keeps an audit trail CSV with RecordHash, UniqueId, source file, timestamp. Backs up each processed source into a date-stamped folder. Logs warnings and alerts to an alert log.
