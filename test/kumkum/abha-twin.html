The Diagnosis: What Drains India's Vitality?

To increase HALE, we must attack the root causes of disability. The report identifies three critical "Drainers" affecting the youth.

The Silent Epidemic

10.6% of Indian adults suffer from mental morbidity. The crisis is particularly acute in urban metros (13.5%). For youth, anxiety and stress are major contributors to "Years Lost to Disability" (YLD), yet the treatment gap remains approx 85%.

  • 1
    Highest prevalence in 15-29 age group.
  • 2
    Driven by exam stress, jobs, and rapid urbanization.
