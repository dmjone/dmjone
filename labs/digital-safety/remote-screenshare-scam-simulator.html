Remote Screen‑Share Scam Simulator
Show, don’t tell: This page simulates the common “customer support / refund” scam. The caller pushes you to install a remote‑access app and grant permissions so they can watch the screen and capture OTPs / UPI PINs. Nothing leaves your browser; it’s an offline demo.

Step 2 — App Permissions
Giving these permissions lets the attacker see your screen, read OTPs, and even tap buttons.
Safe habit: Only allow remote‑help apps from official support inside the app you trust. Never for refunds/KYC.
