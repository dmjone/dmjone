Cyber Awareness Training Hub

Formal, classroom‑ready simulations for Indian users. Lightweight pages that run entirely in your browser — suitable for schools, NGOs, and rural trainings.

  • No backend servers. Uses localStorage only.
  • Hindi/English labels, large buttons, high contrast.
  • Focus: UPI/QR scams, parcel fee frauds, cookie consent, screen‑share risks.
Data handling (please read)
  • All inputs stay in your browser; nothing is uploaded.
  • Use the in‑page Reset to clear localStorage between groups.
  • These pages are simulations for awareness — not real banking/courier services.
If money is lost to a scam in India, call 1930 quickly to attempt freezing the transfer.

About these simulations

Each page is a single HTML file using Bootstrap 5 (via cdnjs) and simple JavaScript. They are designed for “show and tell” in classrooms and community sessions, including rural areas with intermittent connectivity.

To verify or contribute, review the open‑source code on GitHub. You may mirror the pages on your school/NGO website.
